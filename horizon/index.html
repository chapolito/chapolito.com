Horizon

Mobile apps · 2025–2026

Horizon is a synchronous social gaming platform and the backbone of Meta's Metaverse. My primary focus was on core social, privacy, and identity features of the app and gaming experience.

Highlights:

  • Leading a team of 4 designers to define the identity system for integrating Horizon Games into Facebook and Instagram — aligning that work with VPs and Directors.
  • Leading a team of 3 designers to redesign in-game communication (text chat, voice, and emotes).
