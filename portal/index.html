Portal

2019–2021

Portals are ambient smart devices that focus on making video calls with friends, family, or coworkers amazing.

Over the 3 years I worked on Portal, I owned many parts of the software. I designed and launched the first versions of the Facebook Watch, Facebook Live, Workplace Live, and Notes apps, shipped Assistant-powered communications experiences, and was the lead designer for our Identity systems, authentication, and parental controls.

Detailed case studies available by request: jesse@chapolito.com
